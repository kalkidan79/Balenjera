Wellness Tips
  • Box Breathing Technique

    Inhale 4 sec → Hold 4 sec → Exhale 4 sec → Hold 4 sec. Repeat for calm.

  • Grounding Exercise

    Name 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.

  • Better Sleep

    Aim for 7-8 hours. No screens 30 min before bed. Read or listen to calm music.

  • Hydration Reminder

    Drink water when you wake up, before meals, and before bed. Dehydration affects mood.

  • Movement Break

    Even 5 minutes of walking or stretching can reduce anxiety significantly.
